Prashant Mohota

President, Indian Spinners Association

In a significant development during the Annual General Meeting on September 22, 2023, Prashant Mohota, the Managing Director of Gimatex Industries Pvt. Ltd., has assumed the Presidency of the Indian Spinners Association. Gimatex Industries, renowned for its integrated textile plants in Hinganghat near Nagpur and Ahmedabad, boasting 225,000 spindles, sees Mohota's election as a testament to his industry leadership.
