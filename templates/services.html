Activities of ISA / Services Offered

  • Market update on yarn prices
  • Update on price trend of PSF, VSF, and other fibers
  • Liaison between government and industry
  • Policy-related inputs and various textile impacting schemes
  • Trade statistics on import/export
  • Representing spinning industry in one voice
  • Arranging study tours
  • Regular engagement with fiber manufacturers to ensure a fair and transparent price of raw materials
  • Helping spinning mills to contest anti-dumping investigations being launched by fiber industry from time to time
  • Benchmarking on important industry inputs
  • Giving pre-budget memorandum to government on budget proposals
  • Continuous engagement with the Office of Textile Commissioner and Ministry of Textiles on various industry-related issues including payment of subsidy under Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (TUFS)
