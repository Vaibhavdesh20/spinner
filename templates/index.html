About Indian Spinners Association

HISTORY IS COVERED IN OBJECTIVES SENT. STILL I REPEAT BRIEFLY. ISA WAS FORMED IN 1985 BY LEADING SPINNERS LIKE RAJASTHAN SPINNING & WVG MILLS LTD, ADITYA MILLS, BANSWARA SYNTEX ETC. THE OBJECTIVE OF ISA WAS TO PROTECT THE INTEREST OF SPINNING MILLS USING MANMAADE FIBRES AND NATURAL FIBRE. DURING THOSE DAY MAN MADE FIIBRES CARRIED VERY HIGH DUTIES AND AVAILABILITY WAS ALSO AN ISSUE. INDIA WAS A CONTROLLED ECONOMY AND THERE WERE TOO MANY REGULATIONS TO BE COMPIED WITH. EXPORT IMPORTS WERE ALSO HIGHLY REGULATED. ISA TOOK ALL NECESSARY STEPS TO REPRESENT TO CENTRAL GOVT ABOUT VERIOUA HURDLES FACED BY SPINNERS AND SUPPORT NEEDWD FROM GOVT. ISA REPRESENTED TO GOVT ABOUT POLICY CHANGES NEEDED TO HAVE SMOOTH BUSINESS FLOW, INCLUDING EXPORT AND IMPORTS. ISA TOOK BUSINESS DELEGETTIONS ABROD TO APPRAISE YARN USRES ABOUT INDIA 'S POTENTIAL AS YARN EXPORTERS.
