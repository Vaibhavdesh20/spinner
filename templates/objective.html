Objectives of ISA

  1. To engage on a continuous basis with raw material vendors (PSF, VSF, and ASF) to ensure that they charge a fair price to spinning mills.

  2. Helping mills in contesting anti-dumping investigations initiated at the instance of local raw material manufacturers.

  3. To give budget proposals to the Central Government.

  4. To continuously engage with the Textile Commissioner on matters related to the Technology Upgradation Fund.

  5. To engage with DGFT and Ministry of Commerce on matters related to Foreign Trade.
