Objectives ISA

  1. To engage on continuous basis with raw material vendors (PSF, VSF and ASF) to ensure that they charge fair price to spinning mills.
  2. Helping mills in contesting Anti dumping investigations initiated at the instance to local raw material manufacturers.
  3. To give budget proposals to Central Govt.
  4. To continuously engage with Textile commissioner on matters related to Technology Upgradation fund.
  5. To engage with DGFT and Ministry of commerce on matters related to Foreign Trade.
